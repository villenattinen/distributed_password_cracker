protocol:
MAGIC NUMBER + CLIENTID + COMMAND + PAYLOAD

COMMANDS:
CLIENT -> SERVER:
Request: PING       Response: PONG
Request: JOB        Response: ACK_JOB

WORKER -> SERVER:
Request: JOIN       Response: ACK
Request: RESULT     Response: ACK

SERVER -> WORKER:
Request: PING       Response: IDLE/BUSY
Request: JOB        Response: ACK_JOB
Request: SHUTDOWN   Response: ACK
